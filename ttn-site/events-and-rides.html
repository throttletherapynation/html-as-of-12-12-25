home base

Wildcat Offroad Park Events.

Wildcat is our foundational host location and the place where our community grows in real time. Every year we build a mix of group rides, family events, clinics, cleanups, and unity weekends that reflect both rider needs and park realities.

Community Events With Heart.

Whether you’re rolling in solo, pulling up with a full crew, or bringing little future riders, there’s a place for you in the lineup at Wildcat.

Monthly Group Rides

Beginner, intermediate and advanced rides curated so you never feel in over your head — or bored. We ride in color-coded groups with clear expectations and support.

Family & Youth Moments

Events intentionally shaped for Future Riders, parents, caregivers, and families who need pacing and environments that honor where they’re at — not where social media thinks they should be.

Safety & Education Clinics

Powered by Bentley GOALs Offroad and the TTN™ National Rider Education Center: new rider basics, gear walkthroughs, terrain awareness, group-ride etiquette and real-world scenarios.

Community & Cleanup Days

Cookouts, bonfires, trail-cleanup days and networking meetups where riders, park staff and partners collaborate to keep Wildcat safe, welcoming and ride-ready.

non-negotiables

What We Expect At Every TTN™ Event.

TTN™ is about safer, smarter, more healing-oriented throttle therapy. Our events will always reflect that. Whether you’re here for recovery, community, or pure fun — these expectations help protect every rider and every story on the property.

Respect The Land & Staff.
Wildcat and every future host park are not just backdrops — they’re partners. Respect staff, rules, signage, closures and the land itself so we can keep building, not breaking, opportunities for riders.
Safety Gear Is Non-Negotiable.
Helmets, harnesses, appropriate clothing and machine-appropriate safety measures are required. We ride with the understanding that we’re protecting future rides, not just the one we’re in.
No One Rides Alone.
TTN™ events work to create small-group structures, buddy systems and communication plans so no one is left feeling lost, ignored or unsafe — on the trail or in the parking lot.
Hearts Over Highlights.
Content is fun. Legacy is better. We build spaces where survivors, families, veterans, new riders, women and future riders feel seen — not used for clips.

Every TTN™ event is rooted in our mission to help riders Ride. Heal. Belong.™. If an idea doesn’t align with that, we adjust it — or we don’t run it.
