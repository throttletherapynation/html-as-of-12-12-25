What to Include So We Can Respond Well.

You don’t have to write a novel, but a little clarity goes a long way. Use the subject lines above and include as much of the following as you’re able:

  • Your name and role (guide, instructor, owner, manager, educator, director, etc.).
  • Your park, company, program, shop or organization name.
  • Website or main social profile.
  • Where you’re located and who you primarily serve (families, veterans, new riders, etc.).
  • Which TTN lane you’re interested in (Individual Educator, Park Partner, Staff Training, Education Program, etc.).
  • Why you feel aligned with Ride. Heal. Belong.™ and the heart of TTN.
  • Any specific ideas, needs, or questions you want us to consider.

A Quick Word About Boundaries & Capacity.

TTN is intentionally built to grow in a way that still protects riders, families and the people carrying the work. That means we may not be able to move as fast as every opportunity that comes in — and we won’t say yes to educator or park partnerships that don’t align with the heart of the Nation, even if they look impressive on paper.

If you don’t hear back immediately, it doesn’t mean your story doesn’t matter. It means we’re honoring the weight of what’s already on the table while we continue to build what’s next.

If you’re a brand, supplier, sponsor or mentored partner, please use the separate TTN Industry & Brands Gateway so educator and park applications don’t get buried.

If You’re Here, You’re Already Carrying a Lot.

Whether you’re leading rides, running a park, designing staff training, or quietly teaching future riders in the background — you’re part of why this Gateway exists. We can’t promise every door will open right away, but we can promise we’ll treat your story, your time and your people with respect.
