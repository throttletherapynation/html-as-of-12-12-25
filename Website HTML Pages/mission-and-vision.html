mission

What TTN Exists to Do.

Mission: To equip riders, families, parks, and partners with the clarity, tools, and community they need to move through off-road life more safely, honestly, and together — without losing the joy and therapy that brought them here in the first place.

vision

Where We’re Intentionally Heading.

Vision: A Nation of riders, families, parks, and builders who understand the variables, honor the risk, protect each other, and build communities where people can Ride, Heal, and Belong — on and off the mountain.

core values

How We Live This Out: Ride. Heal. Belong.™

ride

The therapy starts with the throttle.

We honor the part of people that comes alive in the dirt, rock, mud, and dark. We teach riding that is rooted in skill, respect, and clear communication — not ego or pressure to send more than a rider or machine is ready for.

heal

We know people carry more than the machine.

Many riders and families are here because life off the trail has been heavy. We create spaces where grief, stress, and transition can be acknowledged without being exploited — and where movement, clarity, and community help people keep going.

belong

Nobody should feel like an outsider here.

New riders, seasoned leaders, widows, kids, park owners, builders — everyone brings something important. We work to make sure language, expectations, education, and community designs never leave people guessing whether they’re welcome. They are.

integrity

We don’t say what we can’t stand behind.

From education to mapping to gear recommendations, we filter everything through real-world experience and responsibility. If we wouldn’t stand behind it with our own family, we won’t put TTN’s name on it.

parks & industry

What This Means for Parks, Builders & Brands.

TTN is not here to be “influencer marketing.” We are here to honor the weight parks and companies carry when they put their names, machines, and land in front of riders and families. When you partner with TTN, you’re aligning with a Mission & Vision that takes that responsibility seriously.

for parks

Clarity, respect & shared responsibility.

We value parks who set clear expectations, care about mapping and signage, and genuinely want riders to understand the terrain they’re entering — not just sign a waiver and hope for the best.

for brands & builders

Integrity over hype.

We look for partners who prioritize safety, long-term performance, and honest communication about what their products can and cannot do. TTN Trusted Gear reflects those standards.

If This Resonates, You’re in the Right Place.

If this Mission & Vision matches what you’ve lived, what you’re carrying, or what you want to help build next, you’re already part of why TTN exists. The next step is simply choosing how you want to plug in — as a rider, a family, a park, a builder, a leader, or all of the above.
